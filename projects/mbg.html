Overview

Mother B Green cleaning company is wanting an update to their website. The owner found SquareSpace cumbersome to use. They wanted the page to be easier to navigate and look more modern with bright colors.

Purpose & Context

This project was a professional page a client hired me to complete.

User Stories:

As a user, I want to be able to find what the services the cleaning company provides.

As a user, I want to be able to contact and get a quote for service.

As a user, I want to be able to access reviews and examples of homes the company has cleaned.

Objective

Revitalize the clients website to make it look more modern and fresh

Approach

Technologies: CSS, SquareSpace design tools

  • Met with the owner to plan what they wanted
  • Set up the structure to the website
  • Added styling
  • Edited the content
  • Went of the styling again
  • Made upgraded page live on SquareSpace

Challenges

This was my first project working with a client. It could be challenging when I liked a certain look or design, but the client had their own ideas of how the website should look. It could take a couple conversations to make their idea clear. I also needed to update the content, and since I do not have any writing experience that was definitely a challenge. I went through several drafts before I liked the flow of most paragraphs.

This was also my first time using SquareSpace to create a website. The owner already had the website set up, so they wanted to stick with this service. It took some hours playing with the design tools to really understand how to manipulate each part and how to best utilize the tools they offer and custom CSS.

Even though the client had thoughts about the design, I was still given lots of freedom to work within their ideas. I enjoyed playing with the colors and layouts to make the clients ideas come to life.

Duration

This project took around a month and a half to complete. I worked part-time on it and there was some back and forth with the owner to get exactly the look they wanted.
